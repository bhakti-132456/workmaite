# ADHD Coping Frameworks

Strategies focused on task initiation, low executive function days, and breaking hyperfocus.

## Task Initiation (Getting Started)
- **The "2-Minute Rule":** If a task takes less than two minutes, do it now. Don't add it to a list.
- **Micro-Stepping:** Break the task down until the first step is ridiculously easy. E.g., Don't "Write the report." Just "Open the word document and type the title."
- **Body Doubling:** Working alongside someone else (virtually or physically). Their presence acts as an anchor, reducing the urge to switch tasks. *This is why WorkmAIte has the Focus check-ins!*

## Managing Focus
- **Pomodoro Modified (25/5):** 25 minutes of intense focus, 5 minutes of rest. During the 5 minutes, you MUST step away from the screen.
- **The "Mirror" Check-In:** Set a recurring alarm every 2 hours. When it goes off, ask yourself: "Am I clenching my jaw? Are my shoulders tense? Have I drank water?" Somatic awareness resets cognitive spiraling.

## Breaking Hyperfocus
- Hyperfocus feels productive, but often leads to burnout and neglecting bodily needs.
- Set hard "stop" times using external smart lights or loud alarms across the room.
